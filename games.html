Genshin Impact

A pair of "star traveler" twins who visit different worlds arrive in Teyvat and witness the destruction of Khaenri'ah. While attemptiong to flee the chaos, an unnamed god blocks their path and separates them: She captures one and seals away the power of the other. The sealed twin, the Traveler, awakens 500 years later and is accompanied by Paimon. They begin the search for the traveler's missing sibling.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite.

  • The game has so many pretty characters and a thrilling gacha system.
  • Each character, element, nation and item has their own well-crafted storyline which makes them all unique.
  • It's free-to-play, so players can play the game however they like.
  • There are lots of different puzzles and mechanisms hidden in the game so you'll never run out of content to discover.
  • The game regularly gives out generous awards! (real not fake)
